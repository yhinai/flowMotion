# FlowMotion: 90-Second Demo Script

**TARGET LENGTH:** 90 seconds
**TONE:** High-energy, explosive, fast-paced, breathless, exciting
**VISUAL STYLE:** Split-second cuts, screen recordings, final rendered video, live UI interactions

---

**(0:00 - 0:10) HOOK: THE IMPOSSIBLE RESULT**
*Visual: A stunning, fully produced cinematic video plays full-screen. High-quality Veo 3 footage, professional ElevenLabs narration, pumping Lyria music, and clean text overlays.*

**NARRATOR (High Energy VO):**
Look at this broadcast-quality 60-second video. It wasn't made by a team. It wasn't made in Premiere. It was generated autonomously from scratch in *under five minutes*. Welcome to FlowMotion.

**(0:10 - 0:25) THE PROBLEM & THE SOLUTION**
*Visual: Quick cut to a frustrated creator scrubbing a timeline, then to the clean FlowMotion UI.*

**NARRATOR:**
Producing high-quality video for social media takes hours of ideation, hunting B-roll, and painful editing. We built an AI pipeline that clones an entire production studio. You just drop a prompt—or better yet, let our system run completely autonomously on live data.

**(0:25 - 0:40) SPONSOR 1: NEXLA (THE AUTOMATED INGESTION)**
*Visual: Screen recording of the Nexla dashboard showing live data (crypto/weather) flowing. Arrow pointing from Nexla into the FlowMotion backend.*

**NARRATOR:**
We refused to do a basic chat app. So we hooked up **Nexla**. Our "cron-style" agents watch Nexla's live data pipelines for trending news or market spikes. When thresholds hit, Nexla *automatically* triggers the video generation. Zero clicks. Live data in, cinematic video out.

**(0:40 - 0:55) THE ORCHESTRATOR: GEMINI + VEO 3**
*Visual: Fast-forward split-screen: Gemini generating a JSON script on the left, Veo 3 video clips materializing on the right, ElevenLabs audio waves below.*

**NARRATOR:**
That live context hits **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, our AI Creative Director. Gemini writes a scene-by-scene script and orchestrates parallel generation: prompting **Google Veo 3** for stunning visuals, ElevenLabs for voiceover, and Lyria for music. All at the exact same time.

**(0:55 - 1:10) SPONSOR 2: ASSISTANT-UI (THE AI DIRECTOR)**
*Visual: Zoom in on the assistant-ui chat. The pipeline is running. The user types: "Wait, make scene 2 a cyberpunk aesthetic!" The UI updates live. Show the heat-graph lighting up.*

**NARRATOR:**
But what if you want to intervene? Our interactive AI Director, built on **assistant-ui**, isn't a static chat—it's a live control room. Interrupt the pipeline mid-flight to tweak prompts for specific scenes, and watch your generation activity light up the custom heat-graph.

**(1:10 - 1:25) SPONSOR 3 & 4: DIGITALOCEAN + AUGMENT CODE**
*Visual: Remotion timeline rendering out server-side. Augment Code logo flashes next to VS Code typing rapidly. DigitalOcean Spaces URL is generated.*

**NARRATOR:**
Building this massive asynchronous beast was supercharged by **Augment Code's** AI tooling. For the heavy lifting? FlowMotion stitches everything programmatically via Remotion and renders server-side on the **DigitalOcean App Platform**, instantly caching the final MP4 to **DigitalOcean Spaces CDN** for lightning-fast playback.

**(1:25 - 1:30) OUTRO: THE FINAL PRODUCT**
*Visual: Back to the gorgeous, finished AI video playing on a mobile phone screen.*

**NARRATOR:**
From a live data spike to a published, cinematic MP4 in minutes. Your autonomous production studio. This is FlowMotion.

# FlowMotion: 1-Minute Demo Script

**TARGET LENGTH:** 60 seconds
**TONE:** High-energy, fast-paced, breathless, exciting
**VISUAL STYLE:** Quick cuts, screen recordings, live UI interactions, final rendered video

---

**(0:00 - 0:05) HOOK: THE IMPOSSIBLE RESULT**
*Visual: A stunning, fully produced cinematic video plays full-screen. High-quality Veo 3 footage, professional ElevenLabs narration, pumping music, and clean text overlays.*

**NARRATOR (Voiceover - High Energy):**
This entire 60-second, broadcast-quality video? It wasn't made in Premiere. It wasn't made by a team. It was generated autonomously in *under five minutes*. Welcome to FlowMotion.

**(0:05 - 0:15) THE PROBLEM & THE SOLUTION**
*Visual: Quick cut to the FlowMotion UI. The user types a simple prompt into the assistant-ui chat interface: "Create a hype video about today's AI news." Hit enter. Loading states begin.*

**NARRATOR (On camera or fast-paced VO):**
Video production takes hours of scripting, hunting for B-roll, recording VO, and editing. We built an AI pipeline that clones an entire production team. You just drop a prompt—or better yet, let our system run completely autonomously.

**(0:15 - 0:25) SPONSOR: NEXLA (THE AUTOMATED INGESTION)**
*Visual: Screen recording of the Nexla dashboard showing live data flowing. Arrow pointing from Nexla into the FlowMotion backend architecture diagram.*

**NARRATOR:**
We didn't even want to type prompts. So we hooked up **Nexla**. Our "cron-style" agents watch Nexla's live data pipelines for trending news or crypto spikes, and *automatically* trigger the video generation process when thresholds hit. Zero clicks. Data in, video out.

**(0:25 - 0:35) THE PIPELINE: GEMINI + VEO 3**
*Visual: Fast-forward split-screen: Gemini generating the JSON script on the left, Veo 3 video clips materializing on the right.*

**NARRATOR:**
The context hits **Gemini 2.5 Flash**, which acts as our Creative Director—writing the script and shot list. It instantly parallelizes asset creation, prompting **Veo 3** for cinematic clips, ElevenLabs for voice, and Lyria for music.

**(0:35 - 0:45) SPONSOR: ASSISTANT-UI (THE AI DIRECTOR)**
*Visual: Zoom in on the assistant-ui chat. The user interrupts the generation: "Wait, make scene 2 cyberpunk!" The UI updates live. Show the heatmap graph.*

**NARRATOR:**
But what if you want to change something mid-flight? Our AI Director is built on **assistant-ui**. It's not a static chat; it's a live control room. Interrupt the pipeline, tweak a scene, and watch your generation heatmap light up.

**(0:45 - 0:52) SPONSOR: DIGITALOCEAN & AUGMENT CODE**
*Visual: Remotion timeline rendering out, immediately followed by a DigitalOcean Spaces URL being generated.*

**NARRATOR:**
The heavy lifting? Stitched together programmatically via Remotion and rendered server-side on the **DigitalOcean App Platform**. The final MP4 is instantly cached on **DigitalOcean Spaces CDN**. And building this complex, asynchronous beast? Supercharged by **Augment Code's** AI tooling.

**(0:52 - 1:00) OUTRO: THE FINAL PRODUCT**
*Visual: Back to the gorgeous, finished AI video playing on a mobile phone screen.*

**NARRATOR:**
From a live data spike to a published, cinematic MP4 in minutes. This isn't just a video generator. This is your autonomous production studio. This is FlowMotion.

*(Fade to FlowMotion Logo + GitHub URL)*
